Classic of Mountains

The Classic of Mountains is a part of the Classic of Mountains and Seas (山海经 Shan-hai Ching). It contains 5 sections: 南山经(Classic of the Mountains: South), 西山经(Classic of the Mountains: West), 北山经(Classic of the Mountains: North), 东山经(Classic of the Mountains: East) and 中山经(Classic of the Mountains: Central), which mainly records the mountains in ancient geography.

In the account of each mountain, the Classic of Mountains describes the hydrology, landforms, plants and animals, minerals, special products and myths and legends of each place, recording lots of valuable natural geographical information.
