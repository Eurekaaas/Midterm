Classic of Seas

The Classic of Seas is another part of the Classic of Mountains and Seas (山海经 Shan-hai Ching). It can be further devided into 海外经 (Classic of Regions Beyond Seas), 海内经 (Classic of Regions Within Seas), and 大荒经 (Classic of the Great Wilderness)

In the account of each mountain, the Classic of Mountains describes the hydrology, landforms, plants and animals, minerals, special products and myths and legends of each place, recording lots of valuable natural geographical information.
